The Care Governance Deficit

Transforming Social Policy into Auditable Care Metrics in Low- and Middle-Income Countries

A New Governance Paradigm

The Care Governance Deficit introduces the world's first integrated Care Governance Architecture (CGA), anchored on the Geriatric Dignity Index (GDI) and the Developmental Agency Index (DAI). The framework converts human dignity from abstract rhetoric into measurable, auditable and predictive governance intelligence.

Care Governance Architecture (CGA)

Geriatric Dignity Index (GDI)

Focus Area Parameters:

• Autonomy | • Agency | • Psychosocial Legacy | • Protection & Safety | • Participation

Purpose: To provide governments and institutions with auditable metrics for elderly care governance.

Developmental Agency Index (DAI)

Focus Area Parameters:

• Safety Baseline | • Peer Dynamics | • Institutional Equity

Purpose: To create measurable and predictive anti-bullying and safeguarding systems within educational institutions.

Disability Inclusion Index (DII)

Measures: Structural accessibility frameworks and cross-border inclusion indices for persons living with disabilities.

Caregiver Resilience Index (CRI)

Measures: The economic, psychological, and specialized professional wellbeing indicators tracking primary caregivers.

Community Protection Index (CPI)

Measures: Vulnerability scaling, systemic community resilience, and active protection channels within at-risk populations and humanitarian settings.

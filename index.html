About Us

CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd is a registered Nigerian GovTech and Social Innovation Enterprise dedicated to building auditable care metrics, digital governance tools, and evidence-based social protection systems for low- and middle-income countries. We develop measurable frameworks that transform human dignity, care delivery, and developmental outcomes into actionable governance intelligence for governments, universities, development partners, NGOs, and community-based organizations.

Professional Services

CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd provides evidence-based care governance solutions that transform social protection systems into measurable, accountable frameworks for governments and development partners.

1. Policy Advisory

Strategic guidance for ministries and agencies on ageing policy, social protection reform, and dignity-centered governance frameworks.

2. Social Protection Metrics Design

Custom GDI, DAI, DII, CRI, CPI indicator development for national M&E systems and World Bank projects.

3. Monitoring & Evaluation

Third-party M&E for UNICEF, NGOs, and government programs. Real-time data collection and impact verification.

4. Dashboard Development

GovTech dashboards for vulnerability mapping, care quality tracking, and predictive policy intelligence.

5. Capacity Building & Certification

Training for social workers, caregivers, and officials. GDI/DAI auditor certification programs.

6. Safeguard Audits & Deployment

Full GDI/DAI index deployment in schools, care homes, and communities. Child safeguarding audits.

Professional Services

CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd provides evidence-based care governance solutions that transform social protection systems into measurable, auditable, and technology-enabled accountability frameworks for governments, development partners, educational institutions, and humanitarian organizations.



📑 Policy Advisory

Design of social protection frameworks, ageing policy reforms, child safeguarding systems, and dignity-centred governance models for ministries, municipalities, and international development agencies.

📊 Social Protection Metrics Design

Development of measurable governance indices including the Geriatric Dignity Index (GDI), Developmental Agency Index (DAI), Caregiver Resilience Index (CRI), and other auditable care metrics.

📈 Monitoring & Evaluation

Impact evaluations, baseline studies, beneficiary assessments, vulnerability mapping, programme audits, and evidence generation for donor-funded interventions.

💻 Dashboard Development

Interactive policy dashboards, offline mobile data collection systems, predictive analytics engines, and real-time decision-support platforms for institutions and governments.

🎓 Capacity Building & Certification

Training and certification programmes for caregivers, social workers, community health officers, schools, NGOs, and government personnel on measurable care governance systems.

🛡️ Safeguard Audits & Index Deployment

Institutional safeguarding assessments, GDI deployment, DAI implementation, elder protection systems, anti-bullying audits, and dignity risk reporting frameworks.

Founder & Executive Director

Ayomide Idris Sanni

Founder & Executive Director, CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd (RC: 8734346)

Ayomide Idris Sanni is a social policy innovator, public administration researcher, and founder of CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd (Corporate Affairs Commission Registration No. RC: 8734346), a premier Nigerian social enterprise and consulting firm dedicated to advancing institutional facilitation, capacity building, and humanitarian development frameworks.

His work sits at the intersection of social protection, governance analytics, and human-centred technology. He specializes in designing and deploying innovative, mathematically grounded metrics that bridge the critical gap between macro-level social policy design and measurable, localized human outcomes, particularly within low- and middle-income countries (LMICs).

Ayomide is the author of The Dignity of Care: Rethinking How We Support the Elderly in a Changing World, and the principal architect of the emerging Care Governance Architecture (CGA)—an integrated ecosystem of auditable care metrics designed to transform human dignity from a moral aspiration into an evidence-based governance asset.

He is also the creator of:

  • The Geriatric Dignity Index (GDI): A multidimensional framework for measuring dignity, autonomy, and institutional accountability in older adult care systems.
  • The Developmental Agency Index (DAI): A quantitative safeguarding and anti-bullying framework for educational institutions and child protection systems.

Under his leadership, CareBridge'07 is pioneering the application of GovTech and SocialTech solutions to social protection through dashboards, telemetry systems, predictive analytics, and real-time policy intelligence platforms.

Why We Exist

Every year, millions of young professionals migrate to global economic centres in search of opportunities. Behind these journeys are ageing parents, children, caregivers and communities navigating new realities of care and support.

Traditional family systems that once protected the vulnerable are under strain. Yet development conversations often focus on economic indicators while overlooking the human relationships that sustain societies.

CareBridge'07 Dynamic Global Ltd was founded to address this invisible crisis by placing human dignity, care systems, social protection and evidence-driven innovation at the centre of development thinking.

Development is not only about economic growth. It is also about who is cared for, who is left behind, and how societies protect human dignity across generations.
